WISHBONE DAY

Wishbone Day is a social movement organised by people with Osteogenesis Imperfecta (brittle bones) which is a genetic condition that causes bones to break easily Wishbone Day draws awareness to the contributions of people with OI, the challenges of living with OI, and the important ways that people can contribute to our journey to live full and happy lives.
